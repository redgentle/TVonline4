How it’s Designed
Dear Loyal Customer,
We are pleased to introduce our new and improved product to you.
It has been a long discussion within our team and we have made
the final decision on this project and we will review these upgrades:

First, we decided our customer needs a strong Case with Slim look
which can fit to most places and offer quality and stability of the
overall product.

Second, we have completed revamped our power supply adapter
and add a power switch at the back of the device to allow the user
to reboot/reset the box without removing the power supply adapter.
Third, we have added Optical sound that required for some
Sound systems to enjoy high quality sounds.

Fourth, RCU has been completed changed to more reliable,
sturdy and responsive RCU. It also has keyboard option for
our users (KODI) if need be.

Fifth, we will support all the most popular middleware solutions
on the market through apps. However, Nova Middleware will be
fully activated on the TVonline 4 to let the user and operator
experience lighting fast load times and very strong security options.

We have made these changes to have the most advanced, reliable,
and up to date, sleek box with lots of additional interesting features.
We had additionally added a Bluetooth option for multi functions for
the end user, Google voice recognition function and updated OS.

We sincerely hope our loyal customers can enjoy our new
TVonline 4 model, as we have listened to your opinions
in the past and are eager to hear your opinions on this as
well for the future.

Thank you all for the support!
